## Reflection Activity

**1. What does AI-Driven Development mean to you?**  
AI-Driven Development is transformative. It allows developers to focus on designing systems and solving problems rather than memorizing syntax. This represents a shift from a code-first approach to a specification-first mindset, ushering in a new era of software development.

**2. How do you see the future of Human–AI Collaboration in software development?**  
Human–AI collaboration will redefine the role of developers. Those who master AI-Driven Development will be able to build groundbreaking systems in 2026–2027, achieving levels of productivity and innovation beyond traditional approaches. AI will empower developers to create scalable, intelligent, and context-aware solutions that were previously impossible to build alone.
